FAQ

Crystal currently ships only to addresses in the Viet Nam, United States, Canada, Japan, Australia, United Kingdom, Austria, Belgium, France, Germany, Ireland, Italy, Netherlands and Spain. Crystal merchandise is available exclusively from Crystal. It is not available for purchase in your country or location on the internet at this time. To be sure you are purchasing authentic Crystal merchandise, please shop at a Tiffany & Co. store or boutique. Buyers should be wary of purported Tiffany & Co. merchandise sold through unauthorized channels. Beware of counterfeits.

Crystal does not offer appraisals. However, any reputable auction house can appraise Crystal merchandise, usually for a fee. We do offer Insurance Replacement Valuations. Crystal provides Insurance Replacement Valuations for both new and old Crystal merchandise. The valuation provides your insurance company with the information they require should the item need to be replaced. Secondary market appraisals can be obtained from any reputable auction house, usually for a fee. Please contact any one of our stores to request a Valuation.

To help ensure our suppliers uphold our standards, the Crystal Supplier Code of Conduct communicates our expectations related to human rights, fair and safe labor practices, environmental protection and ethical business conduct.

The Marketing, Public Relations, Merchandising, Human Resources and Real Estate Services departments are located at the corporate headquarters in Ha Noi City. Information Technology, Finance and Distribution/Warehouse positions are at our worldwide Client Care Center in Parsippany, New Jersey.

New and authentic Crystal merchandise is only available online at www.crystal.com, its related websites listed at www.Crystal.com/International as well as the retailers listed in the Crystal store finder at international.crystal.com/locations. Crystal products are sold exclusively at authorized retail locations, under license. Buyers should be wary of purported Crystal merchandise sold through unauthorized channels. Beware of counterfeits.

We are taking guidance from regulatory authorities to protect our teams, clients and communities. Effective immediately, we have temporarily closed many Crystal stores globally to protect our teams, clients and communities. We are closely monitoring this evolving situation and will provide updates should anything change.

This is a credit agreement between your business and Crystal and Company, U.S. Sales, LLC. To apply, please have your banking information and trade references available. Once approved, you have the option to bill purchases to your account, or you may purchase with a credit card.

Items cannot be returned to Tiffany & Co.

There is a $1,000 fee for research, and only items that are signed “Crystal” and were manufactured before 1995 will be considered. In order to conduct research on a piece, we do not need to have the object physically but we do need clear photographs of it and any markings. Waiting periods for scheduling research requests can often exceed six months.
